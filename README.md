📊 Project Overview

This project focuses on performing end-to-end sales data analysis using SQL on a chocolate sales dataset. The main goal is to transform raw transactional data into meaningful business insights by applying data cleaning techniques, calculating key performance indicators (KPIs), and performing multi-dimensional sales analysis.

The project demonstrates how SQL can be used to analyze sales performance, identify business trends, and support data-driven decision making.

---

🎯 Project Objectives

- Analyze overall business performance using key sales metrics
- Identify top-performing products and countries
- Evaluate salesperson performance
- Analyze monthly sales trends and seasonal patterns
- Generate actionable business insights from the dataset

---

🗂 Dataset Information

The dataset contains chocolate sales transactions with the following columns:

- Sales_Person – Name of the salesperson
- Country – Country where the sale occurred
- Product – Chocolate product sold
- Date – Date of transaction
- Amount – Revenue generated from the sale
- Boxes_Shipped – Number of boxes shipped

---

🧹 Data Cleaning

Before analysis, the dataset was validated and cleaned using SQL by:

- Checking for NULL values
- Detecting and handling duplicate records
- Identifying negative or unrealistic values
- Removing extra spaces and inconsistent text formatting
- Validating date format and data types

Ensuring clean data improves the accuracy of analytical results.

---

📈 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

The following KPIs were calculated to evaluate overall business performance:

- Total Revenue
- Total Quantity Sold
- Average Order Value
- Revenue per Box

These metrics help measure overall sales efficiency and business growth.

---

🔍 Analysis Performed

1️⃣ Country Performance Analysis

- Calculated country-wise revenue contribution
- Identified top performing and lowest performing markets
- Analyzed sales distribution across countries

2️⃣ Product Performance Analysis

- Identified top selling products
- Compared product revenue and quantity
- Detected high revenue but low quantity products indicating premium products

3️⃣ Salesperson Performance Analysis

- Evaluated sales generated by each salesperson
- Identified best performing sales representatives

4️⃣ Time-Based Sales Analysis

- Performed monthly sales trend analysis
- Identified peak revenue month
- Identified lowest revenue month
- Calculated month-over-month growth rate

---

💡 Key Business Insights

Some important insights derived from the analysis include:

- Identification of top revenue generating countries
- Recognition of high performing products
- Detection of seasonal sales patterns
- Identification of premium products generating high revenue
- Opportunities for improving performance in lower performing markets
